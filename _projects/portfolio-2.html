Multi Dimentional Data Analytics of Road Accidents in India

A statistical data analysis project on Road Accidents of India over a period of 5-7 years using various Python Libraries. The project is performed using Jupyter Notebook and Tableau. The various datasets regarding Road Accidents of India were gathered from Open Government Data Platform (Open GDP)
